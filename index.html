It just kept getting bigger and more obvious. Then a friend of mine chatted me up and while we were talking, he told me about his pastor which happened to be pastor Martins, this was last year (2019). I contacted Pst. Exactly a week later which was on the 4th of January, my sister woke me up and said my goitre had disappeared. I placed my hands there and quickly rushed to the mirror and behold, it was no longer there! Today, I'm free and my neck is restored to normal and in perfect condition. I just say thank you Jesus for healing me.
